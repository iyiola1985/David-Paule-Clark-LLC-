Meet David Paule.

As the CEO and founder of David Paule Clark LLC, David brings hands‑on expertise, field experience, and a personal commitment to every project.

Over the years, David has built a reputation for honesty, craftsmanship, and precision. Every home, renovation, and commercial project is treated as if it were his own, with clear communication and honest timelines.

Clients work directly with David, not a distant corporate office. His approach is simple: listen carefully, plan thoughtfully, and build with excellence. This combination of personal ownership and professional rigor is what sets David Paule Clark LLC apart in the construction industry.
